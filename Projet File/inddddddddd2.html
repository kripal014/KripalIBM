How it listens

Every edit a human would make — done while you're still talking.

01

Filler removal

Ums, ahs, and false starts are found by ear, not by a blunt volume filter, so your real pauses for effect stay untouched.

02

Level matching

Guests recorded on a laptop mic and a studio condenser end up sitting at the same loudness, automatically, take after take.

03

Multitrack sync

Remote co-hosts drift out of sync for all kinds of reasons. Wavelength lines every track back up to the frame.

04

Chapter markers

Topic shifts in the conversation are detected and turned into named chapters your listeners can jump between.

05

Show notes draft

A first pass at notes and timestamps is written from the transcript, ready for you to trim down, not write from scratch.

06

Export anywhere

Send the finished mix straight to your host, or pull stems back into your own DAW if an episode needs a human final pass.
